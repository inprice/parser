Product Description

The Waterpik Cordless Advanced Water Flosser is the most advanced cordless Water Flosser. This rechargeable unit with NiMH battery has a 4-hour rapid magnetic charging system and portable design that is perfect for travel. With an electronic pressure control system and 3 pressure settings, the Cordless Advanced allows you to personalize your water pressure with low, medium and high options. Plus it is waterproof and can be used in the shower.  You can also add your favourite mouthwash. The water reservoir holds 207 ml for 45 seconds of flossing time. Includes 2 Classic Jet Tips (JT-100E) for general use, 1 Plaque Seeker Tip (PS-100E) for use with dental implants and other dental work, 1 Orthodontic Tip (OD-100E) for cleaning around braces, Travel Bag, Travel Plug, Tip Storage Case, Magnetic Charger with 2-pin UK bathroom plug (100-240VAC, 50/60Hz), 2-Year Warranty. The Waterpik Water Flosser helps provide healthier gums, brighter teeth, and fresher breath. Healthier gums and cleaner teeth can help prevent tooth decay and future dental work. The Waterpik Water Flosser is perfect for anyone with braces, dental implants, crowns, bridges or periodontal pockets. The Waterpik Water Flosser removes up to 99% of plaque from treated areas and is up to 56% more effective for improving gum health vs. inter dental brushes.

The Power of Water

Volume + Pulsation + Pressure

For better oral health, it is essential to remove plaque and debris from between the teeth and at the gumline, and to massage the gums. To do this effectively, you need the the unique combination of water volume, pulsation and pressure delivered by the Waterpik Water Flosser.

The targeted stream of water removes plaque, food particles and bacteria from areas that other devices can't reach. It also stimulates gum tissue for added health benefits.

Clinically Proven Results

The combination of water volume, pulsation and pressure means that the Waterpik Water Flosser is more effective than air flossing, interdental brushing or string flossing for removing plaque and improving gum health.

Designed for Your Oral Care Needs

The Cordless Advanced features special tips designed for different dental issues, including gum disease, braces, implants and crowns.

3 different pressure settings (low, medium and high) let you choose the power that’s right for you.

Cordless Advanced Water Flosser - Proven Technology

  • 1,250 water pulses/minute
  • 3 pressure settings from gentle to maximum clean (3.1-5.1 bar)
  • Right sized to deliver the power and proven results in a cordless design
  • Ergonomic handle with non-slip grip

Easy to Use

Simply fill the reservoir with water, point the tip at your gumline and floss. Fill with warm water for sensitive teeth or add your favourite mouthwash.

For First-Time Users:

Lean low over sink and place tip in mouth. Aim the tip toward gumline, and turn the unit on.

Glide tip along gumline and pause briefly between teeth, starting with the back teeth and working toward the front.

Continue until you have cleaned the inside and outside of both the upper and lower teeth.

Direct the jet stream at a 90-degree angle to your gumline.

Slightly close lips to avoid splashing but allow water to flow freely from mouth into the sink. Keep unit upright during use for best results.
