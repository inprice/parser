by adaburdet_9 11 Aug, 2018 Top favourable review

Excellent little sound bar! Both in terms of build and sound quality :)

This is a great sound bar. It sounds great and can get very loud for its size. Connected to my phone via Bluetooth, it can honestly fill my whole house with audio xD you could hold a party with this thing and nobody would complain. It sounds great at both low and high volumes and has a surprising amount of bass for its size. It's one of the nicest looking sound bars I've ever seen. I wanted something that wouldn't look out of place and too much like a geeky gadget, and this with the wood on the top and the nice opaque material front, with the brushed metal logo in the middle and the black gloss sides, it's perfect! The build quality is excellent too, it feels solid, nothing rattles, the feet do a good job at keeping it from slipping and the volume knob/combined on/off switch is feels nice and premium, with a smooth continuous actuation and a satisfying click when it passes the on/off point. If you like the look of this sound bar, but you're worried about sound quality, buy it, you won't be disappointed. For the price it's a great buy. Should also state I have no affiliation to the brand, I bought the item myself and this is an honest review of what is an excellent product.
