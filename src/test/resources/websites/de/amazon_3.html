Produktbeschreibung

Introducing the Victrola Pro USB Bluetooth Turntable Vinyl-To-MP3 Recording. A 2-speed belt drive fully automatic turntable with auto lift and return operation. The diamond tip needle provides clear detail, and with the built-in pre-amplifier and RCA output, you can easily connect the Pro USB turntable to your home stereo. Also you can conveniently record your favourite vinyls to MP3. Simply connect the Victrola Pro USB Turntable to your computer with the included USB cable, and follow the on-screen instructions. This is a great way to archive your favourite records!
