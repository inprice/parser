      Description

      • Bonus Local Delivery on in-stock TVs 65" and larger

      Featuring a 3840x2160 resolution and delivering immersive audio, the LG UM76 4K UHD LED LCD AI ThinQ Smart TV brings a breathtaking and cinematic entertainment experience when watching your favourite movies and TV shows.

      Key Features
      • Built with UHD/4K panel for crisp and detailed visuals when playing compatible multimedia content*
      • Tru Motion 100 with backlight scanning helps present movement smoothly
      • Conveniently access various online streaming services, including Netflix and Stan**, via webOS Smart TV
      • DTS Virtual:X technology to bring surround-like sound
      • Supports Google Assistant on your compatible smart device^, thanks to AI ThinQ
      *Native 4K content required for full 4K resolution effect. SD, HD content will be upscaled to near 4K resolution. Membership subscriptions, data usage charges and minimum internet speeds required when streaming 4K content, otherwise content will be downscaled to either HD or SD quality based on available bandwidth. Please check with your internet service provider to confirm the bandwidth for your connection.
      **Netflix requires a steady internet connection speed of at least 25mbps to stream 4K Ultra HD content, otherwise content will be downscaled to either HD or SD quality based on the available bandwidth.
      ^Wi-Fi network and Google Account required. Use of Google Assistant and AI ThinQ required consent to Google and LG Terms of Use and/or Privacy Policies. Controlling devices and features requires compatible smart devices. Internet usage charges and conditions may apply. Subscriptions may be required to access certain content. Features and services may be changed without notice.
