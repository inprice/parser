With a massive 10kg capacity, this top load washing machine is perfect for growing families. Plus, you can even add forgotten clothes after the cycle has already begun.

  • 10kg Clothes capacity
  • 6 Wash programs and 8 water level settings plus 9 water power levels
  • 3 Star Water rating

  • Durable stainless steel drum
  • Ultrasonic vibrating pump
  • Child lock protection
  • Dimensions: 626 × 601 × 985mm
  • 24 Month Warranty

Kogan offers a range of premium appliances that are built to last. Enjoy the latest technology and a modern design without the hefty price tag. Shop the whole collection here.

6 Intuitive wash programs

Whether you need a heavy-duty clean for your doona or delicate underwear wash, the Kogan 10kg Top Load Washing Machine’s large stainless steel drum, six wash programs (Normal, Delicate, Heavy Duty, Blanket, Wool and Custom) and customisable water power and levels will deliver tailored cleaning for every load size.

The ultrasonic vibrating pump helps to remove stains and dirt without additional stain remover or detergent by emitting thousands of vibrations per minute.

Smart Fuzzy Logic and Memory Function settings

Use Fuzzy Logic control for Normal, Cotton and Heavy Duty programs to help reduce water consumption and wash times, while the Memory Function auto-selects the program used last time.

Safe and sturdy

The child lock ensures no little fingers make any unwanted adjustments to the washing cycle.

Features

Multiple Programs

Delay Start Function
