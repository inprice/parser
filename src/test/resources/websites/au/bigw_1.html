Product details

The Repco Blade 20 boys 50cm mountain bike will help take your riding to the next level, with the inclusion of easy to use twist shift 6 speed gearing and Shimano rear derailleur. This adventure-ready bike also features a sturdy steel frame with suspension fork, twin linear pull brakes for better stopping, and alloy rims for a smooth ride.

Product Features:

  • Steel MTB frame
  • Front suspension fork for comfort and control
  • 6 speed with twist shift gear change
  • Shimano rear derailleur
  • Twin linear pull brakes for better stopping
  • Alloy rims
  • 3 piece crank
