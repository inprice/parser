Description

MAKE PERFECT FAVORS FOR YOUR BIG DAY!
Mint to Be candy favor labels for wedding, shower, or party favors. Our personalized stickers are easy to apply directly over retail labels (no need to peel off original labels). High quality, permanent self-adhesive labels, in Matte White or Kraft Brown.

CUSTOM DESIGNED FOR YOUR EVENT
Personalized labels feature "Mint to be" design with your custom names and date, in crisp black script with a red heart accent. Fresh modern calligraphy design is repeated on front and back. Arrow on top makes labels easy to center when applying, and shows recipient where to open the box! Stickers are available in Matte White or Kraft Brown.

MINTY FRESH FAVOR IDEA
Each order includes 12 labels, 3.75" x 1.4375" to fit standard 1oz Tic Tac boxes. Add personalized stickers to mint boxes for a treat your guests will appreciate- include in hotel welcome bags, wedding party baskets, shower favors or thank you gifts.

Listing is for STICKERS ONLY. Mints are not included in purchase price.

HANDCRAFTED IN SEATTLE.
Clementine Wedding & Party is a one-woman operation in Seattle, WA. Each item is designed and crafted with love to provide a personal touch to your celebration. Thanks for supporting a handmade artisan!
