    Great computer but with a big exception

    Great computer but with a big exception

    |
    Posted 1 month ago.
    Pros mentioned:
    Instructions

    I bought this on sale for $800. Once I set this up, I immediately noticed not complete freezes, but just hangups at the login screen, opening programs and loading games. I tried everything I could think of like driver updates, program re-installs, power settings, etc. I finally contacted Cyberpower. Cyberpower customer service was very responsive. Stuck with me through multiple suggestions, but I continued to have the problem. Finally, my opinion is that AMD StoreMI technology is the culprit that is causing the hangups. I can't prove it, but I don't know what else it could have been. The only way to remove it that I'm aware of is to wipe the drives and do a complete re-install of windows 10. I should point out I never had done this myself but Cyberpower has a really good step by step for reinstalling windows on a PC that has had StoreMI installed. Following those instructions and re-installing Windows on the SSD without StoreMI took me maybe 45-60 minutes and a friend helped me with bringing the HDD back online, which was pretty simple too. After doing this my computer runs really, really well. I gave the computer 3 stars because I think for the price I got it at, not having to build my own and how it currently runs, it is great. I took two stars away to raise some caution that this PC may not be for some folks that don't want to tinker with things. I've read some really good things about StoreMI, but for some reason in this case, I believe something isn't right with it.
