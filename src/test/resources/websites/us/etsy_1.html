Description

Wood Wall Art build from 3D triangles from reclaimed wood hand painted, woodburned, cut, dyed and sanded. Each piece is painted in unique acrylic or wood dye colours mixed by me, and not available in any shop, making the sculpture unique and one of a kind. E
Wood pieces are painted in a special way that after assembling I create unique shape/design/picture, similar to the one pictured, unless you choose custom colours. Once the art ios hang, the light is giving different shine by creating shade on each of mine pieces differently and giving a different look from the room.

Previously sold piece on the pictures will be made to order

size on the picture 24 x 55" (60cm x 140 cm)

Colours - walnut, yew, antique pine, light oak, grey, driftwood, off-white, yellow, orange shades or custom colours

Comes ready to hang with high quality hanging hardware for heavy arts

Wood pieces can be hang side by side to fill out larger wall space. Sculpture is smooth to touch and oil is given to add this perfect protection to it.
