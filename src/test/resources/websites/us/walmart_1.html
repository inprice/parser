    Great 12x12 instant canopy bundle!

    I ordered the 12x12 canopy with 2 foldable chairs bundle. The canopy itself is very huge. I read some other reviews before I purchased this canopy, and some people were saying its not 12x12 possibly 9x9. Well I was okay with that because of the great price that also included chairs. But when I set it up to make sure everything was included and nothing was broken, it appeared to be very large and when I measured it I got 12'3 x 12'3 @ the sides of the canopy so with the slant leg design @ the bottom of the legs would be apprx 14x14. So I believe they may have been sent the wrong canopy to begin with. Well as far as the design and setup, the setup is very easy took maybe 5min total. Now after I set it up I can shake the whole thing pretty easily so this may be a problem in high wind situations, I however didn't use the stakes to hold it down, I believe w/the stakes in securely wind may not be an issue. The chairs are not the most comfortable but are very light and sturdy and come with a carrying bag, also the canopy comes with a nice bag with rolling wheels. Overall I would definitely recommend this canopy and chair bundle. Thanks for reading my review have a great day.
