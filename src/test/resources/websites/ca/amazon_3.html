Product description

Because the nursery can also look modern and stylized, this changing table with removable changing station from the Cuddly collection is ideal for all your needs. Its clean lines and attractive wooden legs, cut partly on an angle, make it the perfect complement for baby's room, giving it a contemporary look and lots of storage space. The rounded corners and practical long handles will help create a cozy, safe environment that will grow along with your little angel, thanks to the removable changing station, allowing for a transformation into a chest. Long, elegant nickel-finish metal handles - practical and oh-so-accessible. Wooden legs, cut partly on an angle to emphasize the contemporary style of this piece. Drawer fronts, legs and handles feature rounded contours for added safety. 2 drawers, for the storage space a nursery needs. 2 open storage spaces - ideal for placing baskets or for anything you need to keep within easy reach. A hole for passing cables through, so you can better manage the cords of devices such as a baby monitor or a small radio. Perfect for babies of up to 30-Pounds. Metal slides for smooth gliding. Changing station dimensions: 32,5-Inch (L) x 16,75-Inch (D). Product dimensions: 35,5-Inch (L) x 19,5-Inch (D) x 36,75-Inch (H). Box dimensions: 7,75-Inch (L) x 20,5-Inch (W) x 47-Inch (H). Box weight: 111lbs. Safety is an integrated part of our values: this product meets or exceeds all North American safety standards. Mattress and accessories not included. This product's packaging has been tested and is certified to reduce the risk of damage during shipment.
