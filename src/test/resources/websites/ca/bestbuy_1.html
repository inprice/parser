Overview

Capture those fleeting and once-in-a-lifetime memories in picture-perfect detail with the Nikon D7500 DSLR camera. It delivers exceptional DX performance with a 20.9MP CMOS sensor and EXPEED 5 image processor for 4K UHD video and up to 8fps continuous shooting. DX power

  • Capture exceptional images with amazing details, colours, and textures thanks to the 20.9MP DX-format CMOS image sensor. This sensor lets you take advantage of the smaller, lighter DX NIKKOR lenses, making this an ideal camera for travel.
  • With the EXPEED 5 image processor, the Nikon D7500 is a low-light powerhouse. Boasting a native ISO sensitivity of 100 - 51,200 and expandable to 50 - 1,640,000 (equivalent), you won't have any difficulty shooting in challenging low-light situations.

DX Performance

  • With great power comes exceptional performance. The D7500 lets you shoot true-to-life video in 4K UHD (3840 x 2160) at up to 30fps, so you can forever capture those fleeting, once-in-a-lifetime moments in perfect detail.
  • Action waits for no camera. But the D7500 shoots up to 8fps, so you'll have no problem capturing all of the action.

Creative freedom

  • Great images are for sharing, and with built-in Wi-Fi you can easily share all of your favourite moments with friends and family.
  • Creativity isn't limited to what's at eye level. The 3.2-inch tilting LCD touchscreen lets you take photos from nearly any angle, so you can capture images from your unique perspective.

A lens for lots of shooting situations

  • The included AFS-DX NIKKOR 18-140mm f/3.5-5.6 ED VR lens offers a versatile zoom range for a variety of situations. Shoot a portrait of your spouse in front of the Rockies or a spectacular landscape shot of Lake Louise.
  • Four stops of vibration reduction image stabilization help compensate for camera shake to make your shots come out sharp and blur-free.
