Overview

Apple TV 4K lets you watch films and programmes in amazing 4K HDR — and now it completes the picture with immersive sound from Dolby Atmos.3 Stream your favourite channels live. Enjoy great content from apps on the App Store.4 And thanks to Siri, you can control it all with just your voice. Find sport, breaking news, and complete live-channel and on-demand packages.

What you need

  • A TV with HD capability or better5
  • HDMI cable for HD streaming, or Dolby Vision-compatible HDMI cable for 4K HDR streaming
  • Wired or wireless network
    See Networking
  • Broadband Internet connection (fees may apply)

Technical support and the Apple TV warranty

  • Every Apple TV comes with complimentary telephone technical support within 90 days of your Apple TV purchase. In addition, Apple TV and all accessories are covered against defects for one year from the original purchase date by a limited hardware warranty.
  • You can also visit the Genius Bar at any Apple Store. There you can get advice, insight and friendly hands-on technical support for your Apple TV. Make a reservation ahead of time to guarantee your place.
  • Learn more about the Genius Bar and the Apple Store
  1. 1GB = 1 billion bytes; actual formatted capacity less.
  2. Siri Remote is compatible with Apple TV 4K and Apple TV HD.
  3. Not all content is available in 4K or 4K HDR. 4K resolution requires 4K-capable TV. Accessing Dolby Atmos features requires a Dolby Atmos–capable system. Playback quality will depend on hardware and Internet connection.
  4. Subscription may be required to access some content.
  5. Requires HDCP when playing protected content. Supports up to 4K HDR or Dolby Vision TVs.
